GITHUB REPOSITORY. 

BLUE AND GOLD AI AND LAW-TECH TRANSFER RESEARCH 2022-2026.

Website address: https://github.com/BLUE-AND-GOLD-AI-AND-LAW/BLUE-AND-GOLD-AI-and-Law-Tech-Transfer-research-2022-2026.

BLUE AND GOLD AI and Law-Tech Transfer research 2022-2026 is a public repository of Public Information gathered by article 6 Mexico's Constitution proceedings before INAI and Transparency for the People authorities, between 2022 and 2026.

The order of the information is as follows:
1.	ZIP Files from Technology Development records, from The Big Five federal universities in Mexico (UNAM, IPN, CINVESTAV, UAM, TECNM), and the 32 State Universities (Part I and Part II).
2.	Excel files of summary of patents, copyrights, and labor relations schemes.
3.	PDF file from a consolidated information of the 5 Big Federal Universities in Mexico, The Big Five, (2022).
4.	Public information from Mexico's Senate and Mexico's Congress regarding AI regulation projects (2024).
5.	Public information from CONAHCYT-SEHICTI (2024), case RRA 10604/24 before INAI (September 2024).
6.	Public Information related to Legal Informatics, AI and Law and Legal Education Courses in Public Universities of Mexico (2026).
7.	TFCA Court Statistics Report (March 1-31, 2024).
8.	APPENDIX A, legal case titled “Overcoming the generational gap”, with the answers from the students.
9.	APPENDIX B Database of AI and Law Legal Informatics 2026 PNT petitions (Part 1 to Part 7, and Excel File consolidated).
10.	APPENDIX C Database of Tech-Transfer 2022-2023 PNT petitions to state universities. (2023).
11.	APPENDIX D Database of Professors Positions and paid jobs in University of Veracruz (UV), PNT petitions (2024).
12.	APPENDIX E Letter to Mexican Senate (AI Committee), dated Feb 6th, 2026.
13.	Mexican AI Act draft (April 20th, 2026).

The public repository linked in APPENDIX B, APPENDIX C, and APPENDIX D, database records (2022-2026), and the Cases proceedings before INAI, Transparency for the People and IVAI can also be consulted in the National Transparency Platform in https://www.plataformadetransparencia.org.mx/.
